About Us

Our journey started back in December 2018, when we decided to celebrate New Years’ with young orphans. Our first visit was to Mother Teresa Orphanage, where we went around spreading our idea. Subsequently, contributions came pouring in the form of volunteers and money to carry on this endeavour. That embarked our journey.

Our Goals

With the onset of this initiative, we have embarked on a bewitching journey of celebrating togetherness and spreading joy. Every face that we brighten up, is a redemption of all our hardwork. To relish every festival with one and all, is our raison d'être ' kyunki asli maza toh sabke saath hi aata hai '!

About

We are a team focussed to aid orphanages, specially-abled children and children of slums. Our senior members of the society - living in old-age homes - share stories to help us learn, which we incorporate with ideas from young minds to set goals for our organisation and provide the best possible aid to the society.
